--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BubbleWord is designed to work primarily offline. We do not require you to
create an account or provide your name, email address, or phone number to play.

The App may store the following locally on your device (for example via device

  • Settings preferences (sound, music, haptics)

This data stays on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the App.

1.2 Information collected automatically (via advertising & device services)

When you use online features (such as ads or update checks), certain technical
information may be collected by us or our partners, which may include:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID / IDFA, subject to

  • IP address and approximate location derived from network data
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, rewarded video completion)
  • Diagnostic or crash-related information if provided by platform services


2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve BubbleWord
  • Save your progress and preferences on your device
  • Show advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Process in-app purchases (via Google Play / Apple App Store)
  • Check for app updates where applicable

The App may use internet connectivity to:
